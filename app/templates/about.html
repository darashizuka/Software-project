Welcome to FlaminGo
Our marketplace provides a space for student artists, designers, home cooks, thrifters, and sellers of all kinds to showcase and sell their unique and beautiful products.

Looking to buy?
Whether you're looking for a one-of-a-kind handmade creation, a gently used item, delicious homemade food, educational materials, or quality secondhand furniture and electronics, our marketplace has something for everyone. Not only will you be supporting local talent and promoting sustainability, but you'll also have the opportunity to find some truly special treasures, great deals, and gain valuable insights from our talented students.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FlaminGo?

FlaminGo is a digital marketplace for buying and selling handmade and vintage items.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign up" button on the top right corner of the website and fill in your details.

How can I sell on FlaminGo?

To sell on FlaminGo, you need to create an account and then go to the "Sell on FlaminGo" page. From there, you can create listings for your items and manage your shop.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support by going to the "Contact us" page and filling out the contact form.
